Lesson 1: Steel Structure Basics

基础钢结构词汇与表达

00:00 / 02:30
Text: Steel structure is a structure mainly made of steel materials, including steel columns, steel beams, steel trusses, and other components. It is characterized by high strength, light weight, and good ductility.
中文: 钢结构是一种主要由钢材组成的结构，包括钢柱、钢梁、钢桁架等构件。其特点是强度高、重量轻、韧性好。

Lesson 2: Connection Methods

连接方式：焊接与螺栓

00:00 / 03:00
Text: There are two main connection methods in steel structures: welded connections and bolted connections. Welded connections offer good tightness, while bolted connections are convenient for assembly and disassembly.
中文: 钢结构中有两种主要的连接方式：焊接连接和螺栓连接。焊接连接密封性好，而螺栓连接便于组装和拆卸。
